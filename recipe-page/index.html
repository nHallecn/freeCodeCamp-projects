ACHU

Welcome to this ultimate guide on how to prepare the most famous and reliable afrian meal 'ACHU', originated from the North West region of cameroon. Flilow this receipt to prepare this meal

Ingredients

  • Cocoyams
  • Palm oil
  • Vegetable
  • Meat
  • Pepper
  • Water
  • Stalk
  • Egussi pudding
  • Achu banana

Instructions

  1. Boil and peel cocoyams and achu banana
  2. Then pound them into a smooth, stretchy paste and wrap in banana leaves
  3. For the soup, cook meat, cow skin, and stock fish with spices to make a rich broth
  4. In a separate bowl, mix palm oil with kanwa water until it turns yellow
  5. Then gradually add the meat stock while stirring to form the soup
  6. Season with pepper, salt, and Maggi then add the cooked meat
  7. Serve the hot yellow soup with the achu paste with some cooked vegetable and egussi pudding
